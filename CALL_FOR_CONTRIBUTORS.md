# Call for Contributors — QRON

QRON is an experiment in building an **authentic economy**.

We are not optimizing for:
- Growth hacks
- Speculation
- Token hype
- Dark patterns

We are optimizing for:
- Trust
- Verifiability
- Earned value
- Human-first design

## What Exists
- A Foundational Specification
- A canonical demo concept
- Strong non-negotiable constraints

## What Does Not Exist (Yet)
- Funding
- A company
- A roadmap optimized for hype

## Who This Is For
You might belong here if:
- You care about protocol integrity
- You believe ownership should be earned
- You’re uncomfortable with extractive UX
- You enjoy working within strong constraints

## Who This Is Not For
This is not for:
- Growth-at-all-costs builders
- Token-first thinkers
- People uncomfortable with ethical boundaries

## How to Contribute
- Read the Foundational Spec
- Open issues or proposals
- Contribute code, critique, or design
- Respect the invariants

No promises.
No hype.
Just alignment.

If this excites you, welcome.
